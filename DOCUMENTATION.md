# Specific Documentation

What does DAS, etc mean? Open Tetrio, go to handling, and hover over them.
Note: these controls are hardcoded. Really.

Tokens on clear: Horizontal axis for combo

T-spins can only be considered full spins when the correct conditions are met.
T-Spin minis, and ALL other spins (which matches Tetrio's Tetra League season 2)
are counted as mini.

Tokens gained per minute is rougly double of tetrio's APM.
B2B chaining is used.

An all clear increases tokens earned by 15, *after* combo and B2B multipliers are
given. An all clear also gives 2 B2B in addition to if the clearing gave B2B.
